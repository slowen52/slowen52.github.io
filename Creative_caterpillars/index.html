Our Infant-Toddler Program

Caring. That’s what we do. Infants and toddlers will explore developmental milestones, as we nurture and prepare them for preschool. Using our milestone-based curriculum, Creative Curriculum for Infants and Toddlers, intentional planning, and regular assessments, our teachers create experiences to meet the children’s developmental needs.
